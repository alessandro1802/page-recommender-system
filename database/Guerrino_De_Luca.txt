Guerrino De Luca is the chairman of Logitech's board of directors. He served as President and CEO from 1998 to 2008. De Luca holds a degree in Engineering from the University of Rome. From 1995 to 1997, De Luca was President at Claris Corp.[1] In September 1997, he resigned from his role as Executive Vice President of Worldwide Marketing for Apple Computer Inc.[2]  He held that position from December 1996 to August 1997. Guerrino joined Logitech in 1998.[3] One of his main accomplishments was the purchase of webcam manufacturer Connectix.[4] Originally from Italy, De Luca now resides in San Francisco.
